Index About Me Art Collection

I have always loved Art and creative subjects since a child. However I stoped doing Art when I finished
my secondary school education in order to persue other things.
I decided not to persue art as I found it difficult to create for the sake of creating and so I started my degree in Social Work.
Fast forward two years, the job that I am currently at encouraged me to get back into creating art.
Initially I started creating clothing in the hopes to own my own fashion brand one day, I began to lose interest in fashion design because I did not have the money to fund it.
I decided to start illustrating in late 2019 and by early 2020 I was getting asked to illustrate for people.
I then decided to dedicate my time to art and have had the oppurtunity to work for some amazing people and global companies.
